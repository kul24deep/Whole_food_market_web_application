Refund Policy

Whole Foods Market guarantees 100% satisfaction. We will be happy to refund or exchange any product when accompanied by proof of purchase within 30 days of purchase. We do not offer returns/exchanges for products purchased in our Health & Beauty departments without proof of purchase. Your statutory rights are not affected. Contact your local store for refunds.
